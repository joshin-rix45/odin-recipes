Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2-3 lbs. chicken, cut into serving pieces

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.
  2. Simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  3. Remove chicken from sauce and brown in a little oil.
  4. Place on a serving platter and pour sauce over chicken.
  5. Serve hot.
