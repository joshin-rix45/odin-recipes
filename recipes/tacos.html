Ingredients

  • 1 lb. beef, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Remove beef from skillet and set aside.
  5. Heat tortillas in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Fill tortillas with beef, cilantro, lime juice, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.
  7. Serve hot.
