Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 28 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook for 8-10 minutes or until tender.
  3. Drain spaghetti and set aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  6. Add tomato sauce and sugar.
  7. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  8. Combine spaghetti and sauce.
  9. Serve hot.
